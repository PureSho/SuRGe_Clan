SuRGe — Who we are

Founded on June 13th, 2024 — a respectful, competitive community

Welcome to SuRGe (SRG). We're a friendly, competitive clan built on respect and growth. Whether you're here to scrim, learn, or simply hang out with fellow players, you'll find a place and people ready to support your climb.

Thanks to SRGxPureSho for steady leadership and consistent activity — our environment values positivity, teamwork, and constructive improvement. Work hard, stay humble, and you'll earn respect in our ranks.

★ Mission ★

Objective: Strengthen community bonds and elevate SuRGe through teamwork, skill, and respect.

Outcome:

Grow SuRGe in strength, unity, and reputation while fostering a non-toxic place to play and improve.
